1. Please execute our "BirdBrains.Jar" file located in BirdBrains/BirdBrains.Jar, this will launch our program.

2. You will be presented with four buttons; Exit, 1 Player, 2 Player, Credits.

3a. Clicking on "Exit" will quit the program.
3b. Clicking on "Credits" will bring up the Credits Page.
3b cont. Clicking on "Back" will bring you back to the Main Menu.

3c. Clicking on "1 Player" will give you two options "Hillary" and "Trump", clicking on either will start the game, in Player vs. AI mode.
3d. Clicking on "2 Player" will start the game in Player vs. Player mode.

4. You will be greeted with the two candidates. You will be given 3 options of tweets to select from, hovering over it will highlight your option.

5. Selecting a tweet will damage your opponent's "ego".

6. Take turns selecting your favorite tweet, until you or your opponent's ego reaches zero.

7. Upon a victory, or loss, you will be directed to the respective screen. After viewing your screen, you may return to the Main Menu screen.
